I was faced with this question for one of my assignments in my Workplace Communications course. This was a **second** year course that I somehow ended up taking in my **fourth** and final year of my degree, which certainly gave me an advantage when answering the question.

For the people who have not yet started their degree, the question might actually be: "_what even is there to learn?_". Therefore this question often only arises once you start digging into the vast **array** of tech along your journey. At that point, students might start getting discouraged and say things like "_why the heck have we been needing to learn assembly in this day and age!?_". Here is my best attempt at giving an explanation from my four years of experience.
